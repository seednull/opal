# Minimum Requirements

## Directx 12

At least Windows 10 is required.
At least D3D_FEATURE_LEVEL_11_0 is required.

## Vulkan

At least Vulkan 1.1 is required.

## Metal

At least iOS 18.0 is required.
At least iPadOS 18.0 is required.
At least Mac Catalyst 18.0 is required.
At least macOS 15.0 is required.
At least tvOS 18.0 is required.
At least visionOS 2.0 is required.
